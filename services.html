Collecting and Sorting Donations

Volunteer in our warehouse by sorting and boxing up housewares items like dishes, cups, cookware and linens, so they are ready to get loaded onto our trucks and delivered to people in our community.

Delivering Furniture

In 2020, NLFB delivered 15,812 pieces of furniture, including 1,318 beds to 1,071 households consisting of 1,930 individuals â€“ nearly half were children who had experienced homelessness. Most were households with a single mother and children. Over 80% reported household earnings of less than $12,000 a year. Having a furnished home restores dignity, brings pride of ownership, adds confidence, and allows families to stabilize while working toward self-sufficiency.

Building Furniture

We have a woodshop in our warehouse for building essential pieces of furniture like kitchen tables, end tables, book shelves and desks. In the last 3 1/2 years, volunteers have built nearly 1800 furniture pieces, and volunteers are on track to reach their ambitious goal of building 1,000 items this year! Each piece is skillfully assembled and painted by one of our outstanding volunteers. No experience required. Our expert woodworking leaders will work alongside you or your group.
